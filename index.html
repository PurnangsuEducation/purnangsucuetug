What is CUET UG?

CUET UG (Common University Entrance Test - Undergraduate) is conducted by NTA for admission into various universities, including Assam University and its affiliated colleges.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Qualification: Class 12 pass or appearing.
  • Minimum Marks: General - 50%, SC/ST/OBC - 45%.
  • No Age Limit: Anyone can apply.

Exam Pattern

  • Section 1: Language Test
  • Section 2: Domain-Specific Subjects [minimum 1 // maximum 3]
  • Section 3: General Test
  • Marking: +5 for correct, -1 for wrong answer

Admission Process After CUET

After CUET results, Assam University releases a merit list. The admission process involves:

  • Check CUET results & merit list.
  • Apply for counseling on the Assam University portal.
  • Attend document verification.
  • Pay admission fees & confirm seat.

About Purnangsu Education

(Formerly Rudra Library)

Purnangsu Education, formerly known as Rudra Library, is an innovative educational platform founded by Yogi Purnangsu Nath Mazumder. The vision behind this initiative is to empower students, writers, and researchers by providing high-quality academic resources, literary content, and a space for intellectual growth.

Under the leadership of Yogi Purnangsu Nath Mazumder, Purnangsu Education has expanded beyond just books—it now serves as a hub for learning, literature, and guidance. The platform aims to bridge the gap between traditional education and modern learning methodologies, ensuring that students and aspiring scholars have access to structured, well-researched, and reliable educational materials.

From competitive exam preparation (like CUET UG, AHSEC exams, and more) to literary works that celebrate Bengali literature, Purnangsu Education is a one-stop destination for learners, writers, and knowledge seekers. With a mission to enhance education and make quality learning accessible, Purnangsu Education continues to evolve, shaping the future of countless students.

Yogi Purnangsu Nath Mazumder is the Founder & CEO of Purnangsu Education, a visionary dedicated to revolutionizing education and literature. As an author, researcher, and entrepreneur, he has written multiple books, including Sarathi, ASSEB Grammar Dominator, and Silent Words, helping students excel academically. Beyond education, he is deeply engaged in expanding Bengali literature and researching the Nath Sampradaya, working on an ambitious 3,500+ page book to preserve its rich history. His mission is to empower learners, preserve cultural heritage, and create a knowledge-driven society through innovation and dedication
